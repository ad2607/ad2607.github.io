Overview

The World Happiness Report is a landmark survey of the state of global happiness that ranks 156 countries by how happy their citizens perceive themselves to be. The World Happiness Report 2020 for the first time ranks cities around the world by their subjective well-being and digs more deeply into how the social, urban and natural environments combine to affect our happiness.

Analysis

The dataset that I selected is a world happiness survey which ranks countries based on various socioeconomic factors. A yearly report is constructed by the Gallup world poll ranking each country globally. The main outcome from my data analysis was visualising the data in a way that is clear and accessible to viewers.

Design

I identified the key columns neccessary to represent the happiest countries. I used a map view, bar charts and scatter plots within the workbook.

Conclusions & Recommendations

The most difficult part of this ca was finding a suitable dataset to use. Throughout this CA I learnt valuable skills in data analysis. At the start I wanted to visually represent how socioeconomic factors effected countries happiness. Due to various issues outlined above my dashboard in the end was more a visualisation on the rank and happiness across the globe.
